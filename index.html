About DanceWarriors

DanceWarriors Studio is not just a place to dance, it’s where you can learn discipline and creativity of movement — let your passion take the stage! Our instructors bring styles from Bollywood to Hip-Hop, and Freestyle to Contemporary.

Our studio is open to dancers of all levels — whether you're just starting or ready to take center stage. We believe dance empowers expression, health, and joy.

Meet Our Dance Master – B. Kamalesh

With over 7 years of experience in the world of dance, Kamalesh is the heart of DanceWarriors Studio. Trained in Hip-Hop, Contemporary, and Bollywood styles, he brings unmatched energy, discipline, and creativity to every session. His teaching approach blends fun with technique, inspiring dancers of all levels to push their limits while enjoying the journey.
