About Studio

DanceWarriors Studio is not just an place to dance, Were you can learn disipline and creativity of movement— Let your passion take to the stage! . Our instructors bring styles from Bollywood to Hip-Hop, and Freestyle to Contemporary.

Our studio is open to dancers of all levels — whether you're just starting or ready to take center stage. We believe dance empowers expression, health, and joy.

Meet Our Dance Master – B . Kamalesh

With over 7 years of experience in the world of dance, Kamalesh is the heart of DanceWarriors Studio. Trained in Hip-Hop, Contemporary, and Bollywood styles, he brings unmatched energy, discipline, and creativity to every session. His teaching approach blends fun with technique, inspiring dancers of all levels to push their limits while enjoying the journey. Known for his powerful choreography and patient mentorship, Kamalesh has guided hundreds of students to find confidence, rhythm, and their own unique style on the dance floor.
